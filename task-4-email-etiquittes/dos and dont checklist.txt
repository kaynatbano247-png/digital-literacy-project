Responsible Social Media Use (Checklist)
Do’s
* ? Think before posting anything online 
* ? Use strong passwords and enable privacy settings 
* ? Verify information before sharing 
* ? Be respectful and polite in comments and messages 
* ? Use social media for learning and positive networking 

 Don’ts
* ? Do not share personal or sensitive information 
* ? Avoid posting offensive or harmful content 
* ? Do not believe or spread fake news 
* ? Avoid oversharing your location or daily routine 
* ? Do not engage in cyberbullying or online arguments
