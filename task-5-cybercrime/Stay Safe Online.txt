 Stay Safe Online (Checklist for College Students in India)
General Safety Tips
* Use strong, unique passwords for all accounts and enable 2-factor authentication 
*  Do not click on unknown links in emails, SMS, or WhatsApp messages 
* Download apps/software only from trusted sources (Play Store, official websites) 
* Keep your phone and laptop updated with latest security patches 
*  Avoid logging into important accounts on public Wi-Fi networks 
* Always check for HTTPS (secure connection) before entering personal data 

 Financial / UPI Safety (Important)
*  Never share your UPI PIN, OTP, or bank details with anyone 
* Do not approve unknown payment requests on apps like Google Pay or PhonePe 
* Verify QR codes before scanning—fraudsters may send fake payment requests 
*  Use official banking apps like BHIM and avoid third-party unknown apps 

Smart Online Behavior
*  Think before posting personal information (location, phone number, ID cards) 
*  Do not share private photos or documents online 
*  Be cautious of job/internship scams asking for money 

 Report Cybercrime
*  Report incidents at National Cyber Crime Portal: https://cybercrime.gov.in 
* Call Helpline: 1930 immediately in case of financial fraud
